Blithe Man's Blues in Denver, CO

Blithe Man's Blues is a three-man and one-woman blues and jazz band based in Denver, CO. We have been around since 2005. You can find information about where we perform and what kinds of blues we play.

Blithe Man's Blues CD

We have recorded our first CD! You can find us on iTunes and on CDBaby. We even have a few reviews:

"Blithe Man's Blues gives us a taste of what the Blues were meant to be: A tribute to the dirty, downtrodden, but still proud soul of our nation. These guys tell it like it is!" "Blithe Man's is that rarest of all bands, it is a synergistic mixing of sax and violence!"

Blithe Events

We have a full schedule this season. We'll be travelling all through Colorado, including Durango, Steamboat Springs, Boulder, and even Walsenburg! Please join us in our celebration of the Blues.
